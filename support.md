## Common Questions

**Why isn't the globe loading?**

Atlas requires an active internet connection to load map data and events. Make sure you're connected to Wi-Fi or cellular data, then relaunch the app.

**Events aren't updating.**

Events are refreshed automatically every 30 minutes. Pull down on the globe or event list to manually refresh. If events still appear stale, try closing and reopening the app.

**How do I reset the app?**

To reset Atlas to its default state, delete the app from your device and reinstall it from the App Store. This clears all locally stored preferences and alert rules.

**Does Atlas collect my personal data?**

No. Atlas uses anonymous authentication and does not collect names, emails, or other personally identifiable information. See our [Privacy Policy](https://github.com/Daniel-Berger/Atlas-App/new/main) for details.
