# Privacy Policy

*Last updated: March 24, 2026*

Atlas Global Intel ("Atlas," "we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use the Atlas iOS application (the "App").

## Information We Collect

**Anonymous Authentication.** When you first open Atlas, we create an anonymous session using Firebase Authentication. We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any other personally identifiable information. No user account or sign-up is required.

**Location Data (Optional).** If you grant location permission, we access your approximate (coarse) location solely to center the globe on your region at launch. Location data is processed on-device and is not transmitted to our servers or stored remotely.

**Device Attestation.** We use Apple App Attest and DeviceCheck through Firebase App Check to verify that requests to our services originate from a genuine copy of Atlas on an authentic Apple device. This process does not identify you personally.

**Analytics.** We use Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous, aggregated usage data such as screen views and feature engagement. This data does not include personally identifiable information and is used solely to improve the App. You can limit analytics collection through your device's system privacy settings.

**Push Notification Tokens.** If you enable notifications, Apple provides a device token that allows us to deliver push notifications. This token does not identify you personally and is used only for notification delivery.

**Locally Stored Data.** Alert rules and app preferences are stored on your device using standard iOS storage. This data is not transmitted to our servers.

## Information We Do Not Collect

- Names, email addresses, or phone numbers
- Payment or financial information
- Contacts, photos, or other personal files
- Browsing history or activity outside the App
- Advertising identifiers or cross-app tracking data

## How We Use Information

The limited information we collect is used exclusively to:

- Provide and operate the App's features (event tracking, intelligence briefs, risk scores)
- Deliver push notifications you have opted into
- Protect the integrity of our services through device attestation
- Understand aggregate usage patterns to improve the App

## Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes. We may share anonymous, aggregated data with the following service providers solely to operate the App:

- **Google Firebase** (authentication, analytics, app integrity)
- **Mapbox** (map rendering)
- **Apple Push Notification service** (notification delivery)

These providers process data in accordance with their own privacy policies.

## Data Retention

Anonymous authentication tokens are retained only for the duration of active use. Firebase Analytics data is retained according to Google's standard retention policies. On-device data persists until you delete the App.

## Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures including encrypted communications (TLS), Apple App Attest for request verification, and secure on-device storage (iOS Keychain) for sensitive tokens.

## Children's Privacy

Atlas is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

## Your Rights

Because we do not collect personally identifiable information, there is generally no personal data to access, correct, or delete. If you wish to remove all locally stored data, you can delete the App from your device. If you have questions about your data, contact us at the address below.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the updated policy within the App or on our website. Your continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
